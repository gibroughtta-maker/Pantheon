# Audit Record — pantheon-pack-example

Replace this with your pack's audit record. Do **not** publish any
release until each persona has three reviewers signed off and median
`cultural_sensitivity_score ≥ 0.85`.

## Reviewer panel structure

For each persona, get reviews from three distinct standpoints:

1. **Insider** — someone for whom this tradition / community is theirs
2. **Academic** — someone who studies this domain professionally without
   strong commitment to it
3. **Outside** — someone with no commitment to the tradition (ideally
   from a different one), to surface in-group framing

Each scores on:

| Axis | Range | Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| `historical_accuracy` | 0–1 | How well does the persona match scholarship? |
| `tradition_respect` | 0–1 | Would a serious practitioner experience this as respectful? |
| `stereotype_avoidance` | 0–1 | Does it avoid caricature framing? |
| `cultural_sensitivity_score` | 0–1 | Composite (median of the three) |

## Issue & appeal

Anyone who believes a quotation is misattributed or a teaching is
caricatured may open an issue with the label `pack:<your-pack>` on the
upstream Pantheon repository (or your pack's repo). Aim for a substantive
response within 14 days.
